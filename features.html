Financing & Investments

We provide consultancy services for mining tailored financing and investment solutions for mining, energy-transition and infrastructure assets, connecting asset owners with capital and investors with disciplined exposure to real-asset returns.

  • Capital solutions for asset owners, including project finance, reserve-based lending, corporate and equipment facilities.
  • Alternative monetisation such as royalties, streams, net profit interests and offtake-linked prepayments..
  • Equity and quasi-equity structures: strategic minority stakes, joint ventures, mezzanine and convertible instruments.
  • Investment opportunities in natural resources, infrastructure and real estate aligned with energy-transition and ESG objectives.
  • Ongoing portfolio support across valuation, risk management and capital allocation.

Strategic Advisory & Management

We act as long-term strategic advisers and hands-on partners, helping owners and management teams design, execute and manage value-creation plans across the full asset life cycle

  • Strategic planning and capital allocation at asset and portfolio level.
  • Operational optimisation: cost, productivity and debottlenecking across mines, plants and logistics.
  • Commercial and contracting strategy, including offtake, pricing and market access.
  • ESG, licence-to-operate and stakeholder strategy with a focus on resilient, compliant operations.
  • Programme and project management support to deliver agreed turnaround and growth initiatives.

Mergers & Acquisitions

We advise on acquisitions, divestments and public-market transactions in natural resources and infrastructure, from first approach through valuation, diligence, structuring and closing, including IPO and listing readiness.

  • Buy-side support: deal sourcing, screening, valuation, techno-economic due diligence and bid strategy.
  • Sell-side mandates: process preparation, equity story, data room and auction management, buyer engagement.
  • Structuring of joint ventures, farm-ins/farm-outs, earn-ins and royalty/stream versus sell-down alternatives.
  • IPO and listing readiness, including pre-IPO restructuring and dual-track (IPO vs trade sale) processes.
  • End-to-end execution support, from heads of terms through to negotiation of key commercial provisions.

Assets

We specialise in real-asset transactions across natural resources, infrastructure and real estate, focusing on situations where capital, operational and strategic expertise can unlock step-change value.

  • Natural resources:metals and mining (bulk, base and precious), energy-transition minerals, industrial minerals and aggregates.
  • Infrastructure: ports, rail, roads, barging, storage and energy infrastructure supporting resource and trade flows.
  • Real estate: residential, commercial and hospitality assets linked to resource corridors and urban growth hubs.
  • Transaction types spanning acquisitions and disposals, development and re-development, project finance and joint ventures.
  • Flexible mandate across special situations, growth capital and repositioning of underperforming assets.
